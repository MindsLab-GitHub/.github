## About

MINDS Lab is a research laboratory at the **School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering (AIDE), IIT Ropar**. We develop methods at the intersection of machine learning, data science, and applied AI to tackle fundamental and real-world problems across healthcare, bioinformatics, computer vision, and natural language processing.

## Research Areas

| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **AI for Healthcare** | Cancer biomarker discovery, drug response prediction, clinical LLMs, radiology report generation, survival modeling |
| **Bioinformatics** | Protein function prediction, multi-omics integration, antimicrobial peptide prediction, drug-target interaction |
| **Computer Vision** | Whole-slide image analysis, image segmentation, explainable AI for vision, defect detection |
| **Deep Learning** | Self-supervised learning, transformer architectures, knowledge distillation, model compression |
| **Trustworthy AI** | Explainability, robustness, uncertainty quantification, handling noisy and imbalanced data |
| **Graph & Representation Learning** | Hyperbolic embeddings, graph-augmented reasoning, knowledge graphs |
